Earth

A boring place full of plants and life, Earth is a padded resort for people with bland taste. At least there are turtles and taco pizza there.

Mars

Mars is not just red, it is also brown and kinda greyish! There is a large amount of flat places to run, and sharp rocks to clamber up! Safe and fun, Mars is great for families.

Venus

If you like warm, humid weather and don't mind the smell of sulpher and ammonia, Venus is for you!

Mercury

Mercury is a firey gem! It's really firey though. Don't touch anything. Enjoy the barren charred scenery!

Saturn

Saturn has rings. Everybody likes the rings. Whooooo Rings. It also has cool moons with ice and dirt and lava and stuff. Tragically optimistic scientists think there could be life on a couple od them.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the big one. If you go outside right now and look, you will either be able to see it, unable to see it, or hurt your eyes staring at the sun. The sun is not Jupiter. Stop it.

Neptune

Neptune is big, but not that big. It looks kinda green-blue. there really isn't anything special about it. It's the B-/C+ student of planetary gas giants.

Uranus

Nobody really knows much about Uranus because they are really immature and get caught up in making fun of it's name instead of studying it. Too bad, because it's a pretty color and might be awesome.

Pluto

This is a spot for the real super adventure person type. It's not even a planet really! Staying there is like going camping at the bottom of the ocean. It's stupid, but totally go for it, because it's rad if you do.
